🎵 Welcome to BoatBoat

BoatBoat is your ultimate Discord music bot — featuring a personal touch, automatic playlists, live progress updates, and effortless control through menus and buttons.

🌟 Features

BoatBoat offers a modern, easy-to-use experience with no typing needed. Perfect for any server that wants continuous, engaging music with full control.

  • 🎶 Personal Autoqueue - plays from your history!
  • 🔁 Global Autoqueue - fills empty queues with random songs
  • ▶️ Live song progress bars
  • 🎲 Fun gamble feature
  • 🎧 Priority PlayNext option
  • 🧹 Queue management tools
  • 🔥 View top played songs on your server
  • 🔊 Adjustable volume levels

🎛 Available Commands (All via Button Interface)

🎵 Play Song

Search for a YouTube song or paste a direct link. Plays instantly if the bot is idle, or adds to the queue if something is playing.

⏭️ Play Next

Add a song to the front of the queue to be played immediately after the current song finishes.

🧹 Clear Queue

Clears all queued songs in the server's playlist.

📜 View Queue

Shows the current list of songs waiting to play.

⏩ Skip Song

Instantly skips the current playing song and moves to the next one in the queue.

⏸ Pause / ▶️ Resume

Pause the music anytime and resume it later seamlessly.

🔊 Set Volume

Adjust the bot’s music playback volume between 0–100% to match your server’s needs.

🔁 AutoQueue

Automatically adds 15 random songs from global history whenever the queue gets empty — ensuring nonstop music.

🛑 Stop AutoQueue

Stops the automatic adding of songs.

🎧 MyAutoQueue

Creates an autoqueue just for you — from your personal history of played songs!

🔥 Top Plays

See the 5 most played songs across your server with play counts!

🎲 Gamble

Bet imaginary dollars! Roll 1–1000: win big with a multiplier if you roll over 995. Fun way to compete with friends!
